Opening the wormhole

Submitting MORE TIME to The Line resets the exam, removing submitted answers and unlocked puzzles. Submitting GAP YEAR to The Line/The Square opens a wormhole to GPH 2021, which contains either the Forward, Hibernating and Flying South, or World’s End Concert round.

Forward

The Forward round is unlocked when GAP YEAR is initially submitted to The Line/The Square. If other rounds are unlocked by having enough solves in 2021 rounds, the wormhole will open to Forward only if the conditions for unlocking those rounds are not met (see below).

Hibernating and Flying South

(Unlocked after 6 Forward solves)

A Memory

You just remembered…

…this crazy GAP fact: not only is it important what you submit, it’s also important when you submit. The proctor promises you that if you do it correctly and then turn in your exam, you’ll get access to a completely new set of test questions.

Every feeder answer submitted adds a line to A Memory corresponding to the seconds hand of a clock at the time the answer was submitted. When five or more answers are in the same quadrant, and GAP YEAR is submitted to The Line/The Square, the wormhole opens to the Hibernating and Flying South round. The color of the quadrant corresponds to the season that is unlocked: green for spring, yellow for summer, orange for fall, and blue for winter.

World’s End Concert

(Unlocked after 13 solves in the other two 2021 rounds with at least 4 solves in Hibernating and Flying South)

C L E A R
You just remembered…

…this crazy GAP fact: not only is it important what you submit, it’s also important in what order you submit. The proctor promises you that if you do it correctly and then turn in your exam, you’ll get a CLEARer picture of what’s going on.

Submitting feeder answers adds letters to Another Memory, until five letters are extracted. If the letters read CLEAR and GAP YEAR is submitted to The Line/The Square, the wormhole opens to the World’s End Concert round. The letters extracted are the 8th, 20th, 28th, 40th, 54th of the concatenated alphabetic string of submitted answers, which were picked to allow access even if some answers weren’t found in The Exam. One way to get CLEAR:

  • HALLEY'S COMET
  • STONEWALL
  • GOODBYE, FAREWELL, AND AMEN
  • MORE POPULAR THAN JESUS
