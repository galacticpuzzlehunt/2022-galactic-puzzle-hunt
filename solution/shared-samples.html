Answer EDWARD VIII / CARNEGIE

Written by Alan Huang

When paired up by first letter, the two answers have the same pattern of vowels (AEIOU, always in that order with no repeats). We can read these as five-bit binary, with 1 indicating that the vowel is present. This gives the first answer, EDWARD VIII.

We may have noticed that the clues are sometimes awkwardly worded and are not in any obvious order. They also have no repeated vowels, though here the vowels are not always in AEIOU order. We can again assemble five-bit binary from which subset of AEIOU appears in the clue; this reads WHAT MELLON UNIVERSITY going down the list, which clues CARNEGIE.
