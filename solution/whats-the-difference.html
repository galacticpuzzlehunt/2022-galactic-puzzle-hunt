We may notice the “big things” are provided in alphabetical order (implying that we need to re-sort) and that the “small things” are one letter off from a different image’s “big thing”. We can therefore reorder these items in a cycle.

Reading out the letters from the “small things” that are different from the “big thing” letters gives the answer: MORE POPULAR THAN JESUS.
