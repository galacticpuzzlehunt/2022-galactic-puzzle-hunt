Solution Naval Conflict
Answer LUSITANIA / ARCHDIOCESE

Written by Jenna Himawan

We begin by solving the battleship puzzles:

The final solutions to the puzzles look like this:

The flavortext hints us to find the locations where all 3 grids have a ship. These locations are denoted by the shaded squares in the grid below.

LAEUDSSS
ATSOAVER
ISBTAACN
FRNULHGD
IIAESRIE
CPEEHLOO
APISPCRC
EELLSSEH

This yields the first answer, LUSITANIA.

Now for the 2nd part of the puzzle. If we pay attention to the number of ships that overlap with any given grid cell, the number is either 3 (all ships), 1 (exactly one ship), or 0. It is notable that there are no locations where 2 ships are overlapping but the 3rd is not. Looking at the letters where exactly 1 of the grids has a ship:

Reading all of these in reading order yields a message: OVERLAP IS CHESS BISHOP READ SAFE CELLS.

Now, we imagine that the locations of the overlaps are chess bishops, and mark which cells they are attacking. Attacked cells are denoted with a gray highlight.

Reading the safe cells yields the second answer, ARCHDIOCESE.
