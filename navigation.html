Navigation (Wormhole Simulator)

When you unlock a new round after Forward, the list of puzzles in 2021 changes to show notes next to the wormhole. The first note appears when Hibernating and Flying South is unlocked, after 6 Forward solves. The second note appears when World’s End Concert is unlocked, after 13 solves in the other two 2021 rounds with at least 4 solves in Hibernating and Flying South.
